Tech for Good

What is this?

Tech for Good is an exploratory, community-driven directory for those working in the South Africa tech-industry interested in using their skills for social good.

Our hope is that this page provides the resources required for anyone, regardless of skill or experience level, to jump in and contribute to real-world projects using technology for good.

    • Forthnightly Cape Town Meetup

      Tech for Good uses the forthnightly Codebridge Community Evening as our regular meeting space. This is first and foremost an opportunity for people to relax and connect (there is free pizza and beer!). However, we usually take about 30 minutes (for those interested) to do a catch-up on what everyone is working on and where people can get involved.

      We have a couple of recurring individuals joining in remotely via video/audo chat. If you are interested in joining remotely have a look at our remote meetup instructions.
