Tech for Good

What is this?

Tech for Good is an exploratory, community-driven directory for those working in the South Africa tech-industry interested in using their skills for social good.

Our hope is that this page provides the resources required for anyone, regardless of skill or experience level, to jump in and contribute to real-world projects using technology for good.

    • Fortnightly Cape Town Meetup

      Tech for Good uses the forthnightly Codebridge Community Evening as our regular meeting space. This is first and foremost an opportunity for people to relax and connect (there is free pizza and beer!). However, we usually take about 30 minutes (for those interested) to do a catch-up on what everyone is working on and where people can get involved.

      We have a couple of individuals joining in remotely via video/audio chat on a recurring basis. If you are interested in joining remotely have a look at our remote meetup instructions.

    • Contribute to our community-driven projects

      We have a couple of projects driven by community members. The idea behind these projects is to provide easy entry points for individuals into real-world projects that use technology for good, regardless or experience of skill level.

      Note that if you are interested in getting involved in one of these projects we recommend that you join us (in person or remotely) at one of our fortnightly meetups in Cape Town to learn more suitable projects. However, in the meantime, you can have a look at our Trello board (where we keep track of active projects) and possibly reach out to project owners directly.
