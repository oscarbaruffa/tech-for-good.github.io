Tech for Good

What is this?

Technology for Good is an exploratory, community-driven directory for those working in the South Africa tech-industry interested in using their skills for social good.

This means, first and foremost, that we'd love to merge your pull requests to this repository. New resources, fixes for broken links and typo corrections (among other things) are super welcome!
